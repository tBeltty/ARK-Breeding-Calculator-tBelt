Understanding Server Types

ARK: Survival Ascended offers several official server types, each with different gameplay experiences:

Standard Servers

The default official server experience with balanced rates.

  • Standard progression speed
  • Regular tribe size limits
  • Permanent servers that don't wipe

Small Tribes

Designed for smaller groups with boosted rates.

  • Limited to 6 players per tribe
  • Higher gathering, taming, and breeding rates
  • Designed to level the playing field for smaller groups

Arkpocalypse

Fast-paced servers that wipe periodically.

  • Significantly boosted rates
  • Servers wipe every 30-90 days
  • Faster progression and more intense PvP

Conquest

Hardcore PvP servers for experienced players.

  • Designed for large tribes and alliances
  • Modified rates to balance large-scale gameplay
  • More challenging PvP environment

About Official Rates

Official server rates are controlled by Wildcard Studios and may change during special events. The rates shown on this page are fetched directly from the official ARK: Survival Ascended configuration file. During special events like Evolution Events or holiday events, these rates may be temporarily increased.
